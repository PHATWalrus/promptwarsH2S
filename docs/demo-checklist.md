# Lexguard Demo Checklist

Use this checklist to demonstrate alignment with Problem Statement 01.

1. Register or log in.
2. Upload a PDF/DOCX/TXT agreement or import a public terms/privacy URL.
3. Show the worker progressing through ingest, extract, risk, privacy, explain, and report.
4. Open the contract detail page and point out extracted clauses, risk levels, plain-language implications, scenario examples, and negotiation tips.
5. Search clauses semantically and ask a scenario question in chat.
6. Compare two analyzed contracts and show the benchmark comparison rows.
7. Download a risk report and verify it includes the legal-information disclaimer.
8. Confirm protected routes redirect anonymous users to login.
9. Confirm deployment health with `/api/v1/health` and `/api/v1/health/deep`.
